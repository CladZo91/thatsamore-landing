OUR STORY

Making the perfect
pizza is all about the dough.

That's Amore has been serving authentic Neapolitan pizza in Auckland since 2008. We follow a time-honoured recipe that originates from Naples, where over a century ago Pizzaiolo Pasquale created the original Margherita pizza for Queen Margherita using tomato, mozzarella, and basil, the colours of the Italian flag.

We combine traditional methods with the finest Italian and New Zealand ingredients to deliver the genuine Neapolitan pizza experience. Whether you are dining in or taking away, our focus is on quality and authenticity.
